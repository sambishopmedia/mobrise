About me

Hi, I'm Sam. 

The majority of my life, I have watched movies on the big screen and always been so jealous of the people behind them. Whether they made the soundtrack, or directed the entire thing, I have always wanted to be apart. 

I have always had a passion for music, and am fortunate to be able to sing, play piano, drums, synth, and write for guitar, bass, and other instruments. 

My love for filming and editing started from watching Doctor Who, and immediately shooting my own spin-off with my closest friends. From then, being behind a camera has felt completely natural.

My key achievements include producing an array of professional videos for Corsham Institute, SBM and other media outlets. I have also written, produced and performed on a full studio album, for which I designed the album artwork and booklet for digital and print. I have written a range of screenplays, short films, game stories and developed concepts for mobile apps.

In my spare time, I enjoy shooting travel vlogs, spending time with my family and performing music.

That is basically my life story, in say 5 paragraphs.
