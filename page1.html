MUSIC

Music. What can I say? It is one of the most beautiful things in the world. It can change a mood, it can add meaning, and it speaks louder than words. Below are some examples of songs, instrumental or soundtracks I have worked on. If you can't already tell, I really really love music.

Music for Film

To show the way the non-diegetic sounds can manipulate the audiences' perception of the drama, SBM removed the original sound from this infamous Jurassic Park scene, and completely flipped the mood- changing it from an optamistic, joyous ride, to a negative, scary ride.

Video music by SBM, clip from Jurassic Park.


Music for Performance

As well as writing songs for short films and videos, SBM has also written songs for performance in a range of genres.
'More Than This' was a piece co-written with a musician from Liverpool for her solo work, and SBM wrote an entire album for the duo Larkin, including the songs 'Solace in the Dark' and 'Final Hour' which were made into music videos.

Video music written and co-produced by SBM, shot and edited by DVA Films.
