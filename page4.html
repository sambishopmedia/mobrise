Editing for Promotion

To commemorate the release of the DVD version of Robin Hood: Babes in the Wood Live, I edited an old-action style trailer. I was given the clips without sound, and took the best parts to really persuade the viewer to want to watch the full thing. This trailer was used for promotion of the DVD, and received high praise from the actors and directors involved in the show.

Video edited by SBM.

Editing for Interest

For the Magic and Mayhem Festival in Corsham, I edited a video promoting the whole event, aiming to boost interest and ticket sales. The whole aesthetic needed to be quirky, almost circus-like, to give the title 'Magic and Mayhem' some meaning. This video was uploaded onto social platforms before the event took place, and built interest. 

Edited by SBM.
