VLOGS

Vlogs (or video blogs) are one of the most watched forms of content on YouTube today. Why? Because they are entertaining! Usually filmed on a low budget, edited on a minimal time allowance, and used for either commercial or promotional reasons, they are the advertising tool of the future. Below are some of my own examples of vlogging, used for promotional research.

Los Angeles Vlog

Although the vlog was done for complete personal satisfaction, it allows you to see what can be made in less than a day with just a phone. All edited and shot simply on an iPhone, including music written by SBM. 

This series of vlogs was rather successful due to its interesting style, quick turnaround time, and professionalism on a minimal budget.

New York Vlog

Much like the LA vlog, the style is very unique and it was released the same day it was filmed. The New York vlogs were my first attempt at shooting and editing all on my phone, and it allowed me to see what I am capable of doing with minimal equipment. The style and quality has changed between the NY and the LA vlogs, as my skills and knowledge have progressed.
