VIDEO

Video for Promotion

To help celebrate the Peacock Arts Trail, an exhibition of local artists near Bath, I sat down with Anna Currey, a Children's illustrator, and asked her questions about what to expect and what she has learnt thus far in her extensive career. I interviewed her, but also shot, edited and promoted the video. It is one of my highlights as she had such a fantastic personality. This video was used by the commissioner to help boost ticket sales and to promote the event. Video and editing by SBM.

Video for Interest

To commemorate the launch of the Observatory for a Connected Society app, I sat down with one of its founders, Hans Pung of RAND Europe, for Corsham Institute, and produced this video (sound, lighting and visual). This was my first ever solo project, and this video was used on the day of the launch for promotion and to gain interest from potential investors and users. Video by SBM.

Video for Entertainement

This was a directed piece that was made on a minimal budget, alongside student film-makers and musicians needing experience. The story and the music were shot by students, and Sam directed the piece over the space of two afternoons on two different locations. 

The piece was used in a filming competition and received high acclaim from viewers and judges. 
Directed by SBM.
